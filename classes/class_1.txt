class JobPosition

Attributes:
job_title(string)
hourly_pay(float)
work_hours(integer)
available(boolean)

Methods:
add_job_title(Changes the job_title attribute and gives  title such as: "Waiter", "Cashier")
give_raise(Increases hourly_pay by 10%)
set_work_hours(Gives an integer to work_hours to determine amount of time they will work)
position_available?(Will change the attribute available depending if the position is taken or not)
